About Me

I am a GoHighLevel (GHL) Automation Specialist and Virtual Assistant with hands-on experience in CRM management, system setup, and workflow support.

I help businesses organize their leads, improve follow-ups, and streamline their systems using GoHighLevel. I have experience setting up sub-accounts, using proven snapshots, creating Smart Lists, and managing customer data for better tracking and efficiency.

I also assist with SMS and email campaigns, basic automation workflows, and administrative tasks to support daily operations.

I am detail-oriented, reliable, and focused on building clean and organized systems that help businesses grow.
